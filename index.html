Introduction to NFTs

What is an NFT?

Wikipedia: A non-fungible token is a unique and non-interchangeable unit of data stored on a digital ledger. NFTs can be used to represent easily-reproducible items such as photos, videos, audio, and other types of digital files as unique items, and use blockchain technology to establish a verified and public proof of ownership.

An NFT basically is a unique digital work (image, file, audio, interactive file) that can ber proven to be owned by a person.
