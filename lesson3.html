Turning Ideas into Code

Creating your NFTs

You may want a bit more detailed information about the process and your steps. Let's explore this now.

Art and Creative Stage

  1. Pick a base idea for a single item or a motif for an abstract collection
  2. Decide how you might want to randomize the base
  3. Artistically create each input or component (paper, photoshop, your favorite art creation technique)
  4. Now you have the ideas that you will translate into your code

Image Creation Softwares

For this, you may want to use an image creation software. There are multiple versions you can explore online for free or you can download an editor like GIMP. You can also see if your parents have access to Photoshop or Illustrator.


Coding Stage

  1. Represent all individual elements as code (e.g. pixel art or jpegs to be imported)
  2. Combine all the individual elements with the proper spacing and overlap
  3. Use loops, arrays, random functions and algorithms to generate the art
  4. Now you have the input data for the coding

Working with a team: Coder and Creator

You are able to create these as 1 or a 2 person teams. A coder role and a creator role. Design matters, whether you are doing blocky 8 colour pixel art or hi definition art. Exploring the story behind your art makes it even more interesting for the reader and buyer. Remember there needs to be a reason for someone to spend money on your art, think hard about why your art is the coolest and develop a storyline.
