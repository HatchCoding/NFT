NFT GameJam Rubric

Meeting the Expectations

You will be judged on X elements:

  • Visual Impact
  • Creativity
  • Composition / Design
  • Craftsmanship
  • Relevance
  • Storyline
  • Provenance

For storyline, you will be able to submit a written component with your code to explain why this is a cool piece of digital art. If you make a set of dragon eggs, what world do the eggs live in? Why are these eggs important? What will it mean when the eggs hatch?

Why is it cool?

If you are using a cool algorithm, what’s cool about the algorithm? What was your thought process behind the creation of abstract art? Why is this work unique and special?
