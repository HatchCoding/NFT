Developing an NFT

Why are NFTs cool?

Nyan Cat sold for 600,000 dollars. Think about how much time and energy it took to create Nyan Cat? Why would a single image cost 600,000 and what could you make to start earning money with NFTs!

Let's explore the steps to build and sell NFTs.

Develop an Idea

Before you start, you should think about what digital files you would like to become NFTs. In our case, you will be creating randomly generated art.

Where to Post

After choosing, the general "What" you can start to explore the "Where". There are a few different marketplaces you can choose to post on places like Rarible, Mintable, or OpenSea.

OpenSea is a good starter place as it is free to sign up and simple to get started. Some other companies require you to do a bit more work before posting and selling your art.

Create your Art

This is where you will begin to create your artwork and decide what you are going to do. Will you create a series of people, some cool animal with various outfits, abstract art, GIFs? The choice is yours!

Grab some Cryto and Post your Art

At this stage you will need some money. You will need to buy some currency to pay a "Minting" and the "Gas" fees for your art.

Profit!?!?!

At this stage, if people buy your art, you can make some money. There was a kid who was able to complete exactly what I just explained and made 100,000s of dollars selling his work! If this seems fun and easy we can explore some of the more detailed components.
